How to play

Swipe or use arrow keys to slide tiles. When two matching tiles meet, they merge and evolve into the next stage. Build your way from Brick to Polis!

The stages

BrickAn idea is born
WallYou lay the foundation
GarageWhere startups begin
OfficeThe business takes shape
FortSolo founders protecting a core idea
TowerSmall teams with clear vision
CitadelEstablished firms pursuing growth
PolisA thriving business empire
TAX 5

No one escapes taxes.

Every growing business faces them. Every few moves, a Tax tile will appear and block a cell for 5 turns. You can't move it, merge it, or ignore it — just like the real thing. Plan ahead and build around it.
