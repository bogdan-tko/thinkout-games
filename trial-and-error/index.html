Not enough data? Make your best guess.

How to play

Guess the word in six tries. Each guess must be a valid five-letter word.

After each guess, the tiles will flip to show you how close you are.

The clues

Green — right letter, right spot.
Yellow — right letter, wrong spot.
Dark — letter not in the word.

Tips

The keyboard below tracks which letters you've already tried. Use it to narrow down your next guess.

Start with a word that has common letters — vowels and popular consonants help you eliminate faster.
